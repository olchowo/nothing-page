What do you do…

…when you need to create a webpage with certain features, but you have no content for it?
No main theme, no direction, nothing?
Form should be defined by sense, needs to follow it – and without content there is nothing to follow.
So – since this is a page meant to be about nothing – let's at least make it about colors:
…and this is still just a start.
